Hello! I’m a Ph.D. student in Political Science at Yale University. My research focuses on comparative politics and political economy, with interests in questions of social welfare and public goods provision.

I’m drawn to approaches that combine qualitative fieldwork, statistical analysis, and formal theory. I’m especially interested in what geospatial data can reveal about patterns in political behavior.

Before coming to Yale, I earned a B.A. (Honors) in Political Science and Psychology from Beloit College, Wisconsin.

Outside of research, you can usually find me cheering for the Green Bay Packers (Go Pack Go!), going for a run, or traveling.

If you have any questions about my research, pursuing a PhD in political science, or anything else, please feel free to email me.
